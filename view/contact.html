Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend using our live chat or calling our helpline directly.

What information should I include when reporting a technical issue?

Please include your device type, operating system version, app version, and a detailed description of the issue including any error messages. Screenshots are also very helpful.

How can I schedule a demo of SamaCare for my organization?

For organizational demos, please select "Partnership Opportunity" in the contact form subject line and include details about your organization and requirements. Our partnership team will reach out to schedule a personalized demo.
