General Questions

What is SamaCare?

SamaCare is a healthcare application that helps users assess symptoms and manage medical records in one place. It features a helpful chatbot for medical guidance and keeps track of your health history, appointments, and records securely. Our goal is to make healthcare more accessible and organized for everyone.

Is SamaCare available in my country?

SamaCare is currently available in Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Rwanda, Uganda, and Tanzania. We're actively working on expanding to more countries in Africa and beyond. If SamaCare isn't available in your region yet, you can sign up for our waitlist to be notified when we launch in your area.

Can SamaCare replace my doctor?

No, SamaCare is not designed to replace professional medical care. Our app provides preliminary health guidance, helps organize your medical information, and can serve as a helpful tool between doctor visits. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for diagnosis, treatment, and medical advice. SamaCare is meant to complement your healthcare journey, not replace professional medical attention.

Do I need to pay to use SamaCare?

SamaCare offers both free and premium subscription options. The free version provides basic health assessment, limited medical record storage, and standard features. Our premium subscription includes unlimited storage, priority support, advanced health analytics, appointment scheduling with partner healthcare providers, and additional premium features. Visit our pricing page for current subscription rates and feature comparisons.

Which devices does SamaCare work on?

SamaCare is available on iOS and Android mobile devices through our dedicated apps. You can also access SamaCare through any modern web browser on desktop and laptop computers. We recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge for the best experience.

Account Questions

How do I create a SamaCare account?

Creating a SamaCare account is simple. Visit our website or download our mobile app, then click the "Sign Up" button. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and enter basic profile information including your name and date of birth. We also recommend adding emergency contact information during setup.

Can I create accounts for my family members?

Yes, SamaCare allows you to create and manage family profiles. Premium subscribers can add up to 6 family members to their account. For children under 18, parents or legal guardians can manage their profiles. Each family member gets their own secure profile while allowing the account administrator to help manage records and appointments.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a password reset link. For security reasons, this link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance.

How do I delete my account?

We're sorry to see you go. To delete your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. You'll need to enter your password to confirm. Please note that account deletion is permanent and removes all your data from our servers. We recommend downloading your medical records before deleting your account. The deletion process takes up to 30 days to complete.

Features Questions

What are the main features of SamaCare?

SamaCare offers several key features:

  • Health Assessment: AI-powered symptom checker and health guidance
  • Medical Records: Secure storage for all health documents and history
  • Appointment Management: Schedule and track healthcare appointments
  • Medication Reminders: Notifications for medication schedules
  • Health Tracking: Monitor vital signs and health metrics over time
  • Secure Sharing: Safely share records with healthcare providers

Can I use SamaCare offline?

SamaCare's mobile app offers limited offline functionality. You can access previously downloaded medical records and view upcoming appointments that have been synced to your device. However, the symptom assessment, record uploads, and appointment scheduling require an internet connection. Your data will automatically sync once you're back online.

How do I track my health metrics with SamaCare?

SamaCare allows you to track various health metrics including blood pressure, heart rate, weight, blood glucose, temperature, and more. You can manually input these values or connect compatible health devices and apps to automatically sync data. View your trends in the "Health Tracking" section, where you'll find graphs and reports to help you monitor changes over time.
