General Questions

What is SamaCare?

SamaCare is a healthcare application that helps users assess symptoms and manage medical records in one place. It features a helpful chatbot for medical guidance and keeps track of your health history, appointments, and records securely. Our goal is to make healthcare more accessible and organized for everyone.

Is SamaCare available in my country?

SamaCare is a hospital located in Ghana. However, our web app and chatbot can be accessed from anywhere in the world. This means that while our in-person services are only available in Ghana, you can use our digital platform to manage your health records, get preliminary guidance through our chatbot, and access your medical information globally. If you travel abroad, you can show your SamaCare records to healthcare providers for important medical information.

Can SamaCare replace my doctor?

No, SamaCare is not designed to replace professional medical care. Our app provides preliminary health guidance, helps organize your medical information, and can serve as a helpful tool between doctor visits. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for diagnosis, treatment, and medical advice. SamaCare is meant to complement your healthcare journey, not replace professional medical attention.

Do I need to pay to use SamaCare?

SamaCare is completely free for all users. Anyone can access our chatbot and information about health topics without any cost. To access personalized features like storing and managing your medical records, appointment history, and health tracking, you'll need to create a free account by signing up. There are no premium tiers or subscription fees - all features are available to every registered user at no charge.

Which devices does SamaCare work on?

SamaCare is a responsive web application that works on all devices with internet access. You can use it on smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers through any modern web browser like Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge. The interface automatically adjusts to fit your screen size for the best experience.

Account Questions

How do I create a SamaCare account?

Creating a SamaCare account is simple. Visit our website or download our mobile app, then click the "Sign Up" button. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and enter basic profile information including your name and date of birth. We also recommend adding emergency contact information during setup.

Can I create accounts for my family members?

Yes, SamaCare allows you to create and manage family profiles. Premium subscribers can add up to 6 family members to their account. For children under 18, parents or legal guardians can manage their profiles. Each family member gets their own secure profile while allowing the account administrator to help manage records and appointments.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a password reset link. For security reasons, this link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance.

How do I delete my account?

We're sorry to see you go. To delete your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. You'll need to enter your password to confirm. Please note that account deletion is permanent and removes all your data from our servers. We recommend downloading your medical records before deleting your account. The deletion process takes up to 30 days to complete.

Features Questions

What are the main features of SamaCare?

SamaCare offers several key features:

  • Health Assessment: AI-powered symptom checker and health guidance
  • Medical Records: Secure storage for all health documents and history
  • Appointment Management: Schedule and track healthcare appointments
  • Secure Sharing: Safely share records with healthcare providers

Can I use SamaCare offline?

SamaCare is a web-based platform that primarily requires an internet connection to work. The website needs to be online to access your records, use the symptom chatbot, or make any updates to your information. If you need to have your health information available when offline, we recommend taking screenshots of important details or downloading and saving any critical information to your device as a backup.

How do I track my health metrics with SamaCare?

SamaCare allows you to track various health metrics including blood pressure, heart rate, weight, blood glucose, temperature, and more. You can manually input these values or connect compatible health devices and apps to automatically sync data. View your trends in the "Health Tracking" section, where you'll find graphs and reports to help you monitor changes over time.

Health Assessment Questions

What is the SamaCare health assessment chatbot?

SamaCare's health assessment chatbot is an AI-powered tool that helps you evaluate symptoms and provides preliminary health guidance. It asks questions about your symptoms and health history, then suggests possible causes and recommends appropriate next steps. The chatbot is designed to provide educational information, not to replace professional medical diagnosis.

How accurate is the health assessment?

Our health assessment tool provides educational information based on common medical knowledge and symptom patterns. While it's designed to be informative, it cannot diagnose medical conditions with the accuracy of a healthcare professional. The assessment results should be used as a starting point for discussions with your doctor, not as a definitive diagnosis or treatment plan.

Can I share my assessment results with my doctor?

Yes. After you complete the symptom assessment in our chatbot, you can take a screenshot of the results or simply show the information to your doctor during your appointment. The basic information about your symptoms can help your doctor understand your concerns, though remember that the chatbot only provides general guidance and isn't a substitute for medical advice.

Medical Records Questions

What types of medical records are stored in your SamaCare profile?

SamaCare stores a wide range of medical records, including:

  • Doctor visit summaries and notes
  • Lab test results
  • Medication lists
  • Vaccination records
  • Allergy information
  • Basic medical history

How secure are my medical records?

Your health information is protected by your personal account login. We use secure connections (HTTPS) when you access the website, and your password is stored securely. Our team cannot view your personal health details without permission.

How do I share my records with healthcare providers?

SamaCare offers several ways to share your medical records with healthcare providers:

  • Export to PDF: Download your records as a PDF file that you can email or print
  • Show during appointment: Access your records during a doctor visit from the internet
  • Emergency Access: Include essential health information (like allergies or conditions) on your profile that can be accessed in emergencies

You decide what information to share and when to share it.

Privacy & Security Questions

Who can see my health information?

By default, only you and your doctor can see your health information. No one else, including SamaCare staff, can access your personal health data unless they are explicitly granted permission. You control exactly what information is shared, with whom, and for how long. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

How does SamaCare protect my data?

SamaCare implements comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure Login: Your account is protected by password authentication
  • Data Protection: We store your information using security practices
  • Limited Access: Only you and healthcare providers that are authorized can view your records
  • Privacy Controls: You decide what information to share and with whom

We follow industry best practices to ensure your sensitive medical information remains private and secure.

Can I download or transfer my health records from SamaCare?

Yes, SamaCare makes it easy to access your own health information. You can download your medical records as PDF files at any time from your account. These files can be saved to your personal device, printed, or shared with other healthcare providers. This feature ensures you always have control over your health information and can take it with you when needed.
